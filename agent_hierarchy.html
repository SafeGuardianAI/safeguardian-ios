The Relationship Model

  • Nova (Primary): The entry point for users. Orchestrates general assistance and situational awareness. It queries Trek for tactical data and Apex for coordination logic.
  • Trek (Tactical): Specializes in movement, Landing Zones (LZ), and physical mission status. It provides "ground truth" to the Nova agents in the mesh.
  • Apex (Strategic): Manages high-level coordination between multiple devices. It initiates Pattern 3 sessions to negotiate shared plans across the mesh.
  • The Integraph (WorldGraph): The shared "subconscious" of the mesh. It aggregates StateTicks (vitals) and Gossip (topology) so agents can reason about remote peers without active connections.
