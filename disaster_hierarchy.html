Multi-Tier Connectivity Model

  • The BLE Tier (Civilian Mesh): Local users (User A-C) use standard BLE. Their messages hop across Nova cells until they reach an "exit node" or a rescuer.
  • The Radio Bridge (Trek Rescuer): The Trek Rescuer acts as a critical bridge. Their device has BLE (to listen to the local mesh) and WiFi/TCP (to talk to their tactical equipment, like a drone).
  • The UAV/Drone Relay: A drone flying overhead provides a high-altitude relay. It carries advanced hardware like Starlink Satellite or LTE base stations, providing a "Global Jump" out of the disaster zone.
  • Global Reach: Because of this hybrid hopping, an SOS sent via BLE by User A (who has no internet) can travel through the mesh, up to a drone, and across a satellite link to reach an Apex commander thousands of miles away.
